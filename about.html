Who We Are

Team HBV Irvington is dedicated to education, advocacy, and community impact — empowering students to lead the fight against Hepatitis B.

About Team HBV:

Team HBV is a high school and collegiate outreach initiative of the Asian Liver Center at Stanford University . Our chapter focuses on raising awareness about Hepatitis B, promoting vaccination, and reducing stigma surrounding liver disease — especially in Asian and Pacific Islander communities.

The Jade Ribbon Campaign:

The Jade Ribbon Campaign is the global awareness movement founded by the Asian Liver Center. It symbolizes prevention, education, and hope in the fight against Hepatitis B and liver cancer. The jade ribbon represents liver renewal and jade’s cultural importance as a symbol of longevity and health.

What is Hepatitis B:

Hepatitis B is a viral infection that attacks the liver and can lead to chronic disease, cirrhosis, or liver cancer. It spreads through contact with infected blood or bodily fluids but is preventable through vaccination. Education and early testing are essential to eliminating HBV worldwide.
