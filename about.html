About Team HBV Irvington

Team HBV Irvington is a student-led organization working to increase awareness of hepatitis B while providing STEM learning opportunities and community service.

What is Team HBV as an organization?

We are a student group that runs study programs, community outreach, and public health initiatives. Our chapters focus on empowering students with STEM skills while engaging in meaningful service related to hepatitis B awareness and prevention.

Jade Ribbon Campaign

The Jade Ribbon Campaign is an international public health initiative focused on hepatitis B awareness, early detection, and reducing stigma—especially in Asian and Pacific Islander communities where HBV prevalence is higher. The campaign promotes education, testing/screening resources, and culturally-informed outreach. What is the Hepatitis B virus?

Hepatitis B virus (HBV) infects the liver and is transmitted through blood and certain body fluids. It can cause an acute infection and, in some people, a chronic infection that increases risk of liver damage, cirrhosis, and liver cancer. Vaccination is available and highly effective at preventing infection; testing and early care are important.
