About Team HBV Irvington:

The Jade Ribbon Campaign:

The Jade Ribbon Campaign is a national initiative led by Team HBV to raise awareness about Hepatitis B, reduce stigma, and promote early testing and prevention within at-risk communities.

What is Hepatitis B:

Hepatitis B is a viral infection that affects the liver and can lead to serious health complications if left untreated. Many people live with Hepatitis B without symptoms, making education and awareness especially important.
