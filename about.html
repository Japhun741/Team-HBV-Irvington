About Us

About Team HBV:

Team HBV is a collegiate and high school outreach initiative of the Asian Liver Center at Stanford University . Our mission is to increase awareness of the Hepatitis B virus (HBV), promote vaccination, and reduce stigma surrounding liver disease, particularly in Asian and Pacific Islander communities.

The Jade Ribbon Campaign:

The Jade Ribbon Campaign is the global awareness movement founded by the Asian Liver Center. It symbolizes the fight against Hepatitis B and liver cancer by spreading knowledge about prevention, testing, and treatment. The jade ribbon represents liver renewal and the cultural importance of jade as a symbol of health and longevity.

What is Hepatitis B:

Hepatitis B is a viral infection that attacks the liver and can lead to serious conditions such as cirrhosis or liver cancer. It spreads through contact with infected blood or bodily fluids but is entirely preventable through vaccination. Awareness, testing, and early care are key to eliminating HBV worldwide.
