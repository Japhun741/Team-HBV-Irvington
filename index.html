Why Should You Join?

Team HBV Irvington is a student-led chapter of the national Team HBV organization, dedicated to raising awareness about Hepatitis B and liver health while providing meaningful service opportunities for students.

What is Team HBV Irvington?

USABO Prep:

Study sessions and collaboration opportunities for the USA Biology Olympiad.
Service Opportunities:

Participate in impactful volunteer projects that directly benefit the community and spread awareness about Hepatitis B prevention.
Annual Conference:

Attend and network with other Team HBV chapters across the Bay Area to learn from experts and share ideas.

Community Outreach

We collaborate with local organizations to spread awareness about Hepatitis B prevention and liver health. Every event strengthens our bond as a team and as advocates for change.

Education and Empowerment

From classroom presentations to schoolwide campaigns, our members lead initiatives that inform and inspire. Knowledge is power—and we share it with our peers and community.

Student Leadership

Our officers and members work together to plan impactful events, organize fundraisers, and create opportunities for everyone to lead with purpose and compassion.
