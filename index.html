WHO ARE WE?

​The goal of our club is to expose members to pre-medicine and leadership opportunities. As a high school chapter of Stanford Team HBV, we raise awareness of hepatitis B and other easily preventable common diseases among Asians via fundraisers and Stanford guest speaker events. Simultaneously, we cultivate leadership within members by providing oral presentation, youth conference, workshop, and volunteering opportunities.

WHAT IS TEAM HBV?

Team HBV is the outreach arm of the Jade Ribbon Campaign (JRC). Our mission is to increase awareness of the dangers of hepatitis B and liver cancer. We are an international community comprised of collegiate chapters, high school chapters, and local volunteers based out of the Asian Liver Center at Stanford University.

WHY SHOULD YOU JOIN?

  • ​by joining our organization, you will be on the front lines working to educate the international and local community about hepatitis B and liver cancer
  • we give members a bimonthly opportunity to organize a leadership or pre-med workshop with another officer
  • we cover other Asian health concerns such as diabetes, osteoporosis, and cardiovascular disease through scientific research presentations
  • we invite doctors and medical researchers to speak to our club members about their contributions to these diseases
