Who Are We

Team HBV Irvington is a student-led chapter of the national Team HBV organization, dedicated to raising awareness about Hepatitis B and liver health while providing meaningful service opportunities for students.

What is Team HBV Irvington?

  • USABO Prep: Study sessions and collaboration opportunities for the USA Biology Olympiad.
  • Service Opportunities: Participate in impactful volunteer projects that directly benefit the community and spread awareness about Hepatitis B prevention.
  • Annual Conference: Attend and network with other Team HBV chapters across the Bay Area to learn from experts and share ideas.
