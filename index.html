Who are we

Team HBV Irvington is a student-led team focused on education, outreach, and hands-on projects that improve community health literacy and provide real-world STEM experience.

What is Team HBV Irvington

We are a chapter of student volunteers that organize study sessions, outreach, and events centered on hepatitis B awareness and STEM learning. Core activities include:

  • USABO prep — structured biology Olympiad study groups and resources for students preparing for the USA Biology Olympiad.
  • Service opportunities that make a difference — health education workshops, local screening drives, and community outreach projects.
  • Annual conference — a day-long student conference with workshops, guest speakers, and project showcases.
